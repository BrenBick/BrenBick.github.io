Simply Green SFU App

The point of this project was to follow a typical agile development process working on an Android app. We work on the app for 3 sprints, gathering user requirements, implementing features, and updating the project specifications document. The goal of our app was to solve the problem of users not knowing where to properly dispose of items. We used an interactive map and questionnaire to guide users to the proper disposal areas on campus.

In this project, I took on the role of project owner. I was responsible for ensuring that the user requirements were properly address and that we stayed on track during each sprint. Additionally, I also developed the map and algorithms used to find the nearest disposal points to the user. In the final sprint, I was also responsible for unit testing the features that I implemented. Overall, my team ranked 2nd place that semester for our app and received a cash prize.
