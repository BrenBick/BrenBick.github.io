Emotion Perdiction Project

The goal of this project was to predict the emotion depicted in a short video clip using emojis as a visual aid. This was achieved by using a multimodal application of machine learning models. We used one model to evaluate the audio from the video and a second model to evaluate the visual component. Then, the models were combined in late fusion to produce the final prediction.

In this project, my role was to research a way to construct the visual processing pipeline. This involved writing functions to extract image frames from the video clips, building the training data set, and training the convolutional neural network on the data.
